As you can see on the red line, we entered our Put Spread when Silver was in a state of extreme, crisis-driven backwardation. We sold the 8400 strike, expecting that geopolitical premium to hold the front month up. However, the blue line shows what happened by March 12th: the panic faded, the extreme backwardation collapsed, and the front-month price acted as a release valve, crashing straight through our 8400 strike and triggering our structural stop-loss.
